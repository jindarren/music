IntersectionExplorer is an interactive visualization that allows you to explore papers of the IUI 2016 conference. The visualization shows you papers that you have bookmarked in Conference Navigator and users who are similar to you because they bookmarked similar papers. In addition, this visualization enables you to explore recommendations that are generated based on your interests.

You can watch the tutorial or play with this tool.

Key features

  • Agents represent recommendations generated with different recommendation techniques (label 1).
  • You can explore relations between recommendations generated by different recommender agents and bookmarks of users. Tags can also be added to filter out items related to a specific tag.
  • Details of the recommended items can be explored on the right side (label 3).


Explanation

Each row represents a potential overlap between your interests and that of others.

An empty circle indicates that there is no overlap.

A full circle indicates an overlap between your interests and those recommended by agents or other users. This is where you may find interesting papers.

A blue circle shows papers already bookmarked by you.

Participate in a short user study!

To participate in this study, please explore the visualization and bookmark papers that are of interest to you (by exploring bookmarks of other users, by exploring papers that are suggested to you by recommender agents or by exploring the tags.)

The task is very simple: Try to find as many talks as possible that are of interest to you and bookmark them using the bookmarks function on the right side.

You can play with the visualization of the IUI 2016 conference here.

Afterwards, please fill out a questionnaire.

Your participation will be very much appreciated!
